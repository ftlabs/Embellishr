App

FTLabs: Embellishr

The data is loaded into the app via SAPI requests.

Endpoints (NB, all behind SSO. Will be tokenized later.)

  • /api/:year/:word
    • e.g., Full data for 'Europe' mentions throughtout 2016
  • /api/:year/:word?person=person
    • e.g., All data for 'Europe' mentions involving David Cameron throughout 2016
  • /api/:year/:month/:word
    • e.g., Full data for 'Europe' mentions throughtout Febuary, 2016
  • /api/summary/:year/:word
    • e.g., As above, but with a less verbose response.
  • /api/condensed/:year/:word
    • e.g., Minimium view containg the indexCount for each month and associated facets
  • /api/topSearchTerms/:num
    • e.g., top 10 most recently searched for terms
